Our diploma is recognized by members of the [U4 Group of GitHub Research Universities](https://github.com/U4Group); 
and probably all the McDonald's with a [working ice-cream machine](https://mcbroken.com/).

A [diploma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diploma) is a document awarded by an educational institution
testifying the recipient has graduated by successfully completing their courses of studies. 
Historically, it has also referred to a charter or official document of diplomacy.
